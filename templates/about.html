Verification Process

Records submitted to the museum undergo a community verification process. Verified records have established provenance through one or more of the following:

  • Direct acquisition from players, teams, or tournament organizers
  • Documented chain of custody with photo/video evidence
  • Serial numbers or unique identifiers matching official records
  • Community corroboration from witnesses to the item's origin
  • Authentication by recognized experts in esports memorabilia
